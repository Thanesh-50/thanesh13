Waste Segregation Guidelines

Proper waste segregation is important to ensure that waste is disposed of in an environmentally responsible manner. Here are some guidelines to follow:

  • Separate organic waste (e.g. food scraps, yard waste) from recyclable waste (e.g. paper, plastic, glass).
  • Do not mix hazardous waste (e.g. batteries, electronics) with regular waste.
  • Check with your local waste management facility to see if they accept specific types of waste.
  • Follow any additional guidelines provided by your waste management provider.
